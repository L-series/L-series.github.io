About Me

I am incoming PhD student at the University of Montreal. I completed my B.Sc at McGill University in Mathematics and computer science and my M.Sc at the University of British Columbia where my research focused in Analytic and Computational Number theory. I am interested in applying techniques from Machine Learning to problems of Number Theory as well as studying the representations and semantics of written mathematics in language models.
